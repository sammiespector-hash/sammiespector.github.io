Sammie currently leads UX Content Design at Angi. Previous hats include heading up the Content Design team at Condé Nast and a UX Design team at Vogue.

Experience

My UX career grew hand-in-hand with the industry; I was the very first content design intern at Intuit in Silicon Valley. From there, I worked in FinTech at Intuit and Morningstar, Inc. then jumped to Publishing and Interactive Media. Now, I'm working on digital marketplaces for the broadest range of users yet, homeowners and tradespeople across the globe.
